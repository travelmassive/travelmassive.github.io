What Is Travel Massive?

OUR PURPOSE

Travel Massive’s Mission is to unearth and connect travel insiders in every city in the world in order to create a purposeful change to the way we travel. (Yes, that means creating the future of travel)

OUR VALUES

Travel Massive is an open and collaborative community founded on three simple principles – to be open to everyone in the travel industry, to be free to participate, and to connect and share ideas globally. We follow six core values that guide our actions.

Build Meaningful
Partnerships

We want to help our members build meaningful, trusted relationships to help them be more successful. If we can help to build better relationships we can make the travel industry even more successful.

Foster Innovation
And Big Ideas

Our community can help entrepreneurs and thought leaders share disruptive ideas in tourism at a global level to create innovation. We want to help those creating big ideas to have access to partners, resources and support.

Support Diversity
And New Talent

New talent and increased diversity will enable the travel industry’s continued growth and success. We will support initiatives that foster learning, skills development and mentorship within the tourism industry globally.

Empower
Change In Travel

Travel is an incredibly powerful tool for making the world a better place. We want to inspire a change in the way we travel that brings more meaning to people’s lives and that truly benefits everyone on the planet.

Promote
Responsible Travel

The development of the travel industry has a responsibility to ensure that we reduce our harm on the environment, cultures, and local economies. We must consider the future impact of travel and tourism.

Create Happiness
Big And Small

In everything that we do, we want to create happiness for our members and community. What we create with Travel Massive should be rewarding and fun - just like travel itself.

OUR PROJECTS

Travel Massive projects are team-driven initiatives that deliver value to our community and address our core values. Paid projects help to support services that we provide for free to our community.

Travel Massive Community FREE

The foundation of Travel Massive are free and open community events. Community chapters are created by one or more chapter co-founders who volunteer to organize local events to connect and develop their local travel industry. Travel Massive events are non-profit but may be supported by local industry sponsors who assist with event costs.

Travel Massive Platform FREE

Our community platform provides a central place for membership and events world-wide. Members can host their own individual and company profiles, explore and connect with other members, and join their Travel Massive chapters. Our platform is actively developed and released under an open-source license on massiveplatform.com.

Travel Massive News FREE

Through our blog, video channels, and podcast series, our content team documents the achievements of Travel Massive members who are making an impact in travel and to share their story. Our interview series unearths and highlights the incredible achievements of change makers, startups, and innovative companies around the world.

Community Fund FREE

The Travel Massive Community Fund will commit a percentage of revenue back to our community to support projects and initiatives of our members. This fund will be announced in more detail in Q2 2016.

Travel Massive Marketplace PAID

The marketplace is a place for our community to promote and discover tourism and travel industry opportunities such as jobs, partnerships, affiliates, destination marketing, content creation, startup services, investment and more. Learn more at travelmassive.com/marketplace

Travel Massive Workshops PAID

Our workshops provide an open platform for skills sharing that empower our community to share and learn from each other in an equitable way. Experts can earn by providing a workshop for other members to learn a skill.

Travel Massive Partnerships PAID

Sponsored partnerships allow companies to engage with our community via featured articles and custom online campaigns. Sponsorship revenues assists with the funding of our community content and free services.

Travel Massive Ventures Q2 2016

Travel Massive Ventures is a platform for matching startups and entrepreneurs with the right investors, mentors and advisors.

B CORP

Travel Massive is incorporated as a legal entity “Travel Massive Global P.B.C” – a Public Benefit Corporation, based in Delaware. This allows us to commercially fund support of our community projects.

A benefit corporation is a for-profit entity that wishes to consider society and the environment in addition to profit in their decision-making process.

OUR STRUCTURE

We will continually strive to provide a structure that is scalable and transparent, and that allows our community to have a say.
Advisory Board

The advisory board consists of the Founding Investors of Travel Massive who have financially supported the organization to achieve its overall Mission.

Project Teams

Project Teams are are responsible for delivering each Travel Massive Project initiative to the community. Projects are funded based on their own merit.

Project Teams consist of a Project Leader, and one of more team members. Teams regularly report their activities and progress to the Advisory Board.

Chapter Leaders

Chapter Leaders are motivated by creating networking and learning opportunities to benefit the development of their local travel industry.

By founding a local Travel Massive chapter, Chapter Leaders volunteer their time to organize regular events in their cities to connect members of the travel and tourism industry to meet, share ideas, and collaborate at free and open events.

The Community Team provides Chapter Leaders assistance through regional community support managers.

Community Support Managers are responsible for providing guidance and support resources to Chapter Leaders, helping new Chapter Leaders to join, and assisting with finding opportunities such as speakers and sponsors for their events.

Find An Event Near You

Our story. Travel Massive started on the rooftop of a Sydney youth hostel in 2010 as a gathering of curious people who came together to share ideas about the travel industry. The event grew to regular monthly gatherings attracting hundreds of travel industry insiders, startups and content creators who came to create connections and share ideas. Shortly after, two expat members returning home to Toronto and London launched the first international chapters and created the beginning of the Travel Massive movement. The rest is history - and the future.
