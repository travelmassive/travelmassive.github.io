Travel Massive is the world's largest independant, open community for the travel industry.

Our purpose is to connect the future of travel.

We are founded on three simple rules — free to attend events, open to everyone in the travel industry, and to connect and share ideas.

Since our origin in Sydney in 2011, our movement has connected 40,000 travel innovators, leaders and creators in 180 countries world-wide.

We believe in — building partnerships, big ideas, diversity and talent, purposeful travel, positive impact, and happiness. These are our values.
