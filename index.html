#TRAVELMASSIVE
Then, Now, And The Future Of Travel.
Travel Massive started over a six pack of beer on the rooftop of the YHA Hostel in Sydney Harbour in 2010. We met to talk about travel, and these events quickly grew in size each month as more people found out about our meetings that were often hidden in backpacker bars.

Since those days we’ve grown to a global community of thousands of members in over 60 cities worldwide.

We based our meetings on three simple rules – to be open to all the travel industry, to be free to attend, and to encourage people to connect and share globally. These founding principles started the first chapters of Travel Massive in Toronto, London and San Francisco and created the beginnings of our global network in early 2011.

Together we’ve created a worldwide movement that is making an impact in travel.

Wherever you are right now, look out the window and think about why you travel. We travel for adventure, to learn new things, to meet new people, to challenge ourselves, to have new experiences, to see lives from another perspective, to grow ourselves, to reach out and explore, and for a million other reasons. Travel can transform lives and change our worlds.

As pioneers, leaders and influencers of the travel industry, we have a responsibility for influencing how and where people will travel and for creating the experiences they will have.

Innovations and ideas from our community will create new ways to dream, plan, arrive, explore and to share our journeys.

Travel Massive is bringing new ideas, collaboration, innovation, diversity and new talent to the travel industry. We are unearthing innovation around the world, bridging gaps across verticals, connecting people, building new relationships, and fostering collaboration and shared learning about how we can make travel truly better.

We are creating a new era of exploration for a new generation of travelers.

The travel industry is a worldwide phenomenon that involves every country in the world and is one of the largest, most globally integrated industries on the planet.

The global travel industry accounts for 9% of global GDP and 1 in 11 jobs (sources: Abacus Ventures).

Travel Massive connects us because we see travel as a tool to transform people’s lives and as a means of changing the world.

A New Mission With a Purpose.
Travel Massive’s Mission is to unearth and connect travel insiders in every city in the world in order to empower change in travel.

As the world’s largest open travel industry community, we are helping to connect the people, ideas, skills and resources that contribute to creating and developing the future of travel.

Now it’s time for us to have a mission that defines and connects our community with a purpose.

Our new global mission builds on our founding values and guides a path for us to build an organization that can truly make a positive change in the world.

Building on Our Values.
Travel Massive has always been an open and collaborative community founded on three simple principles – to be open to everyone in the travel industry, to be free to participate, and to connect and share globally.

These principles have guided us to now, but as we’ve grown our impact has become bigger. In addition to our founding principles, we’ve created the following Community Values:

  • Building Meaningful Partnerships

    We want to help our members build meaningful, trusted relationships to help them be more successful in their jobs and businesses. Partnerships are the backbone of the travel industry and if we can help to build better relationships we can make the travel industry even more successful..

    Fostering Innovation And Big Ideas

    We want to help the industry develop real innovation and support those who are tackling big ideas that can make fundamental shifts in travel. By providing a collaborative community and network that helps people share ideas and learn, we can foster innovation at a global level. We want to help those creating big ideas to have access to partners, resources and support.

    Supporting Diversity And New Talent

    We want to enable the travel industry’s growth and success with new talent through learning, skills development and mentorship. We also want to develop more diversity through initiatives that widen the representation of the industry and encourage more people to join.

    Empowering Change In Travel

    We want to see that our efforts underpin our mission to improve travel and to make our jobs and roles have a purpose that benefits every person in the world. Travel is an incredibly powerful tool for making the world a better place. We want to inspire a change in the way we travel that brings more meaning to people’s lives and that truly benefits the societies that we visit.

    Promoting Responsible Travel

    Travel impacts the entire world – the development of the travel industry has a responsibility to ensure that we reduce our impact on the environment and local societies. Tourism will grow to a level where the sustainability of certain destinations and attractions must have the access limited. It is our job to see that society as a whole can continue to enjoy travel without risk of damaging our environment.

Key Initiatives
Now that we’ve defined our mission statement, core values, and community values, we can implement initiatives that support these.

Travel Massive Initiatives can be implemented in a number of different ways that determine the nature of the initiative and whether it is a volunteer initiative or a paid-for initiative.

Volunteer Initiative means – Travel Massive volunteers (such as chapter leaders) implement the initiative and are supported by the Global Team (who are supported by for-profit initiatives).

Global Team Initiative means – The Travel Massive Global Team implements the initiative. This means someone is financially paid to deliver the initiative.

Partnership Initiative means – The initiative is implemented via a partnership agreement with a 3rd party entity. The partnership must satisfy the Travel Massive Community Values and Mission Statement. The partnership may apply shared costs as well as for-profit revenue sharing.

Future Initiative means – The initiative is a future idea and and seeking leadership.

The following is a brief overview of Travel Massive Initiatives either being currently implemented or planned.

Initiatives are led by an appointed leader and may be supported by by small teams to deliver the initiative to the community.

  • Travel Massive Community
    (Volunteer Initiative)

    Travel Massive Chapters and events have been the foundation of the Travel Massive movement. Chapter Leaders are local leaders who create and connect the local Travel Massive community through locally organized events. Travel Massive events are non-profit but may be supported by local industry sponsors who pay for the cost of events (such as venue hire, drinks).
    Join the community at travelmassive.com

    Travel Massive Platform
    (Global Team)

    The platform will provide a central place for membership and event management. The platform will allow members to host their own individual and company profiles, explore and connect with other travel massive members, and join their Travel Massive chapters. It will be free for members to sign up to the platform. The platform will be mobile-based and be released under an open-source license.
    Learn more at massiveplatform.com

    Community Partnerships
    (Global Team)

    The Global Team will implement a sponsorship model to provide additional engagement and benefits to the Travel Massive Community. The community partner must align with Travel Massive’s values and mission. The partnership will be managed as a commercial relationship and will deliver sponsorship revenues so that Travel Massive’s Global Team can support our volunteers and community base.

    Travel Massive
    Global Gathering
    (Partnership Initiative)

    Travel Massive Global Gatherings will bring together the Chapters Leaders of our community to meet, learn, and collaborate. Our Global Gatherings will provide an independent event for guiding the future of Travel Massive and provide collaborative workshops and meetings to shape the future of travel. We will work with Travel Massive partners and travel events to host our gatherings.

    Travel Massive
    Innovation Labs
    (Partnership Initiative)

    Travel Massive Labs will provide co-working spaces for our members in key travel innovation hubs around the world to connect travel industry innovators with the world’s leading travel brands and companies. We will partner with existing co-working spaces or labs or develop our own with the support of sponsors where most appropriate.

    Travel Massive
    Road Trips
    (Partnership Initiative)

    Travel Massive Road Trips is an initiative to venture into new regions of the world and connect our members with local tourism and travel industries through new media generation. We will establish partnerships with travel brands and DMO’s (Destination Marketing Organizations) to showcase and explore tourism and travel destinations with leading travel bloggers, writers and filmmakers.

  • Travel Massive Marketplace
    (Future)

    A community marketplace for ideas and requirements in the travel industry.

    Travel Massive Underground
    (Future)

    A collaborative resource for travel hacking and travel technology developers.

    Travel Massive Ventures
    (Future)

    A platform for matching startups and entrepreneurs with the right investors, mentors and advisors.

Travel Massive Structure
Since our origins as a single event with a half dozen people, we’ve grown in size to thousand of members in countries across the world.

Our structure has evolved as a requirement of organizing Travel Massive on a global scale and the necessities of supporting our community and Chapter Leaders. The Travel Massive structure will evolve and adapt as we grow. We will strive to provide a structure that is scalable and transparent, and that allows our community to have a say.

Our organizational structure is based on the following operational groups:

  • Global Team

    Our founders, global community managers, and staff who support our community and chapter leaders on a full-time or part-time basis.

    Chapter Leaders

    Our volunteer chapter leaders who arrange regular events and are the local leaders of the Travel Massive community in their cities.

    Ambassadors

    Members who evangelize Travel Massive’s mission and values and have personally committed to supporting and promoting the Travel Massive movement.

    Advisors

    Individuals who create additional value and opportunities for Travel Massive and our community, through their own networks and their professional associations.

    Global Council

    An elected group representing the Travel Massive community from both individuals and companies who can contribute to the leadership of Travel Massive.

Travel Massive Global P.B.C.
We have chosen a Benefit Corporation as our legal entity, which strikes a balance between providing a free community and funding the resources to support it.

Travel Massive is incorporated as a legal entity “Travel Massive Global P.B.C” – a Public Benefit Corporation, based in Delaware.
